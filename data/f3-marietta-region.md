# F3 Marietta Region

F3 Marietta is a local region of F3 Nation, serving Marietta, GA and the surrounding areas.

- **Launched:** June 2024.
- **Focus:** We are dedicated to bringing the F3 mission to the men of Marietta.
- **Workouts:** We currently have workouts at locations like "The Battlefield" (Marietta High School) and "The Last Stand".
- **Community:** Beyond workouts, we engage in community service, social events (2nd F), and leadership development opportunities.

Come join us in the gloom!
