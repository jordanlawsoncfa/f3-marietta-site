### Definition
A welcome back given to a PAX who has been absent for a long period.

### How it's used
Named after the TV show 'Welcome Back, Kotter.' Used when a man 're-posts' after a hiatus.

### Variations
- Welcome Back

### Notes
Always celebrate a man's return; never shame his absence.
