### Definition
The final 5-10 minutes of every workout where men gather in a circle to close out.

### How it's used
Includes a Count-a-rama, Name-a-rama, announcements, and a 'Shout Out' or prayer to end the session.

### Variations
- Ball of Man (BOM)

### Notes
This is the most important part of the 45 minutes.

### Related terms
- 6th Man
