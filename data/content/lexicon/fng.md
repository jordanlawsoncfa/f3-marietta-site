### Definition
A man attending his first F3 workout who has not yet been given an F3 name.

### How it's used
Treat him with the 'Red Carpet.' He is the most important man at the workout until he is named at the end.

### Variations
- EH

### Notes
Never use the 'F' to mean anything vulgar.
