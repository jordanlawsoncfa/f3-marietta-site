### Definition
The man who takes responsibility for leading the workout or event.

### How it's used
The Q is the 'Quarterback.' He plans the beatdown, starts on time, and leads with confidence.

### Variations
- VQ (Virgin Q)
- Co-Q

### Notes
In F3, we are all leaders; eventually, every man must Q.
