### Definition
The pre-planned path for a run or ruck to ensure safety and timing.

### How it's done
Check for lighting, traffic, and construction before the workout starts. Have a backup plan.

### Variations
- GPS Mapping

### Notes
Always have a 'turn back' point for the Six.
