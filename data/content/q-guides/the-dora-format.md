### Definition
A classic F3 partner workout structure.

### How it's done
Partner 1 performs reps (100 Merkins, 200 Squats) while Partner 2 runs. Switch until done.

### Variations
- Partner Work

### Notes
Great for building 'Second F' during a beatdown.
