### Definition
The process of giving a new man his F3 name at the end of the workout.

### How it's done
Ask about his job, hobbies, and weirdest life stories. The PAX then brainstorm and vote on a name.

### Variations
- Name-a-rama

### Notes
Names should be memorable but never truly insulting.
