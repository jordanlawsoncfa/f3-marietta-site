### Definition
The written report of the workout posted to the digital locker room (Slack/Website).

### How it's done
Record the date, AO, Q, PAX count, FNGs, and a summary of 'The Thang.' End with a 'Moleskin' (commentary).

### Variations
- BB

### Notes
Essential for accountability and 'Second F.'
