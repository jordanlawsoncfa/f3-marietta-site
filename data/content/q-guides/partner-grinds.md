### Definition
Exercises where one partner works while the other performs a 'timer' move.

### How it's done
Example: P1 does 100 Merkins while P2 runs to a cone and back. They switch until the work is done.

### Variations
- Dora 1-2-3

### Notes
Forces 'Second F' (Fellowship) during the 'First F.'

### Related terms
- The Thang
