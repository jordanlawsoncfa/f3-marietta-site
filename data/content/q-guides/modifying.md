### Definition
The ability to offer easier or harder versions of an exercise.

### How it's done
If the Q calls for Merkins, tell the PAX they can do them on their knees or against a wall if needed.

### Variations
- Scaling

### Notes
Ensures the workout is 'inclusive but challenging.'
