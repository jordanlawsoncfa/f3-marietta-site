### Definition
The F3 standard of finishing exactly at the stated time.

### How it's done
Finish the COT by the 45 or 60-minute mark. Men have jobs and families to get to.

### Variations
- The Clock

### Notes
Never run over time; it's disrespectful to the PAX.
