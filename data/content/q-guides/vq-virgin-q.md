### Definition
A PAX member's first time leading a workout.

### How it's done
The PAX will give you grace, but they will also heckle. Focus on clear commands and a solid 'Disclaimer.'

### Variations
- Co-Q

### Notes
Ask a veteran to be your 'Co-Q' for support.
