### Definition
The mandatory statement given at the start of every workout to establish safety and limit liability.

### How it's done
Gather the PAX and state clearly: 'I am not a professional. This is a suggestion. You are here at your own risk. Modify as needed. Most importantly, do not sue me.'

### Variations
- The 'Short' Disclaimer

### Notes
Never skip this. It's the first thing a Q does.
