### Definition
Adjusting the workout for inclement weather.

### How it's done
If it's pouring, move the workout under a 'picnic shelter' or parking deck (The 'Covered' option).

### Variations
- Liquid Sunshine

### Notes
We don't cancel for rain, we just adapt.
