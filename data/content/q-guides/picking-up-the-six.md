### Definition
The leadership practice of ensuring the last man is never left alone.

### How it's done
When the group is running, the faster men must turn back and run with the slowest man until everyone reaches the destination.

### Variations
- Leave No Man Behind

### Notes
This is what separates F3 from a gym class.
