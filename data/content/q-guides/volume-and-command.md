### Definition
Using a loud, clear voice to project commands over the mumble chatter.

### How it's done
Speak from the diaphragm. Your commands should be heard by the man at the back of the line.

### Variations
- Projecting

### Notes
A quiet Q leads to a confused PAX.

### Related terms
- Cadence
