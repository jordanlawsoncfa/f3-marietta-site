### Definition
The trap of a leader who begins to believe the AO exists to serve him instead of the other way around.

### How it's done
We avoid 'Kinging' by constantly rotating the Q and empowering new leaders.

### Variations
- Ego

### Notes
Humility is required for a Nantan or a Site Q.
