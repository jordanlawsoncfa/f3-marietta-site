### Definition
A small, high-accountability group of 3-4 men who share their deepest struggles.

### How it's done
A 'Shield Lock' meets regularly to discuss the 'Third F' and protect each other's blind spots.

### Variations
- Small Group

### Notes
Named after the Spartan Phalanx shield wall.
