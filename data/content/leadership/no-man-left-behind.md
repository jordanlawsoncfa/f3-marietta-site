### Definition
The F3 commitment that no man, regardless of fitness level, will ever be left alone.

### How it's done
The PAX always 'pick up the Six.' If a man is struggling, someone stays with him.

### Variations
- Leave No Man Behind

### Notes
Our strength is measured by the man in the back.
