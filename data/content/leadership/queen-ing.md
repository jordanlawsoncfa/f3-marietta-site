### Definition
A play on 'Kinging' that refers to how F3 improvement affects the M (wife) and the home.

### How it's done
When a man improves, his wife (The Queen) and his home (The Kingdom) benefit.

### Variations
- Home Impact

### Notes
A man's primary AO is his own home.
