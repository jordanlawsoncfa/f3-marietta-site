### Definition
The specific, intentional way we welcome a New Guy (FNG) to his first workout.

### How it's done
Approach him immediately, introduce yourself, stay with him during the workout, and name him.

### Variations
- Welcoming

### Notes
The FNG is the most important man at the AO.
