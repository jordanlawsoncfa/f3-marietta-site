### Definition
The F3 organizational model where there is no 'central' boss; every man is a leader.

### How it's done
Based on the book by Brafman/Beckstrom. If you cut off a leg, it grows back. F3 is a movement.

### Variations
- Starfish

### Notes
F3 is a leader-full organization, not leader-less.
