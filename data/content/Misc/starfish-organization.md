### Definition
The organizational model F3 follows where there is no central 'head' or boss.

### How it's done
Based on the idea that if you cut a starfish in half, you get two starfish. Every man is a leader.

### Variations
- Starfish

### Notes
Prevents the organization from becoming a 'cult of personality.'
