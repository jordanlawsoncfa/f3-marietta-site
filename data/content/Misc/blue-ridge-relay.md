### Definition
An annual 208-mile team relay race through the mountains of North Carolina.

### How it's done
F3 sends dozens of teams (12 men each) to run through the night in a test of endurance and fellowship.

### Variations
- The Relay

### Notes
A premier 'Second F' event for many Regions.
