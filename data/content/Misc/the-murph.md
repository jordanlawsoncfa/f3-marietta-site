### Definition
A famous workout consisting of a 1-mile run, 100 pull-ups, 200 push-ups, 300 squats, and another 1-mile run.

### How it's done
Typically performed on Memorial Day in honor of Lt. Michael Murphy. Often done with a 20lb vest.

### Variations
- Mini-Murph

### Notes
A staple 'CSAUP' event for most AOs.
