### Definition
A perimeter-based workout where exercises are performed at each corner of a field or parking lot.

### How it's done
The PAX run to Corner 1 (10 Merkins), Corner 2 (20 Squats), Corner 3 (30 LBCs), and Corner 4 (40 SSH). After one lap, the Q may change the exercises or increase the reps.

### Variations
- Escalating Four Corners

### Notes
Effective for managing a large group of PAX.
