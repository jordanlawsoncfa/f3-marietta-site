### Definition
A series of short sprints to increasing distances and back.

### How it's done
Line up at a start line. Sprint to the 5-yard line and back, then the 10-yard and back, continuing to the 25-yard line. Touch the line with your hand at every turn.

### Variations
- Plank Suicides

### Notes
Standard agility work often used in the Thang.
