### Definition
A detailed look at how a Q would execute a Four Corners beatdown.

### How it's done
**The Setup:** Use the four corners of the parking lot. **Lap 1:** 10 SSH at each corner. **Lap 2:** 10 Merkins at each corner. **Lap 3:** 10 Big Boy Sit-ups at each corner. **Lap 4:** 10 Burpees at each corner. **The Six:** Fast PAX do LBCs while waiting for the Six.
