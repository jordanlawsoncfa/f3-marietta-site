### Definition
An acronym-based workout where the PAX accumulate reps for five specific exercises.

### How it's done
PAX must complete: 50 **B**urpees, 100 **O**verhead Claps, 150 **M**erkins, 200 **B**ig Boy Sit-ups, and 250 **S**quats. Usually done as a partner Dora.

### Variations
- Modified BOMBS

### Notes
Easy to remember, hard to finish.
