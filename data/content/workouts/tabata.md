### Definition
An interval-style workout focusing on maximum intensity followed by brief rest.

### How it's done
20 seconds of maximum effort (e.g., High Knees) followed by 10 seconds of rest. Repeat 8 times for a 4-minute cycle. Usually several cycles are done.

### Variations
- Weighted Tabata

### Notes
High metabolic demand in a short window.
