### Definition
An exercise set where reps increase with each round.

### How it's done
Perform 1 rep of an exercise, then 2, then 3, up to 10. Can be combined with a sprint between each rung.

### Variations
- Descending Ladder (10 to 1)

### Notes
High volume focus on a specific muscle group.
