### Definition
A ladder-style workout involving two exercises at two different points that always add up to 11.

### How it's done
Perform 10 reps of Exercise A at Point 1. Run to Point 2 and perform 1 rep of Exercise B. Run back to Point 1 and do 9 of A and 2 of B. Continue until you reach 1 of A and 10 of B. Total reps per exercise equals 55.

### Variations
- 7s
- 21s

### Notes
Mathematically simple but physically brutal.
