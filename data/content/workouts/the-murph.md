### Definition
A specific 'Hero' workout performed annually to honor fallen SEAL Lt. Michael Murphy.

### How it's done
1-Mile Run, 100 Pull-ups, 200 Push-ups, 300 Squats, 1-Mile Run. Often performed wearing a 20lb weighted vest or ruck.

### Variations
- Mini-Murph
- Partitioned Murph

### Notes
The ultimate test of volume and endurance.
