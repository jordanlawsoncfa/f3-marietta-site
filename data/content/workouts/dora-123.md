### Definition
A classic partner-based accumulation workout where one man works while the other travels.

### How it's done
PAX pair up. Partner A performs reps of an exercise while Partner B runs to a pre-determined point (like a light pole) and back. They switch roles and continue until the total team reps are met. Typical reps: 100 Merkins, 200 Squats, 300 LBCs.

### Variations
- Dora 1-1-1
- 11s

### Notes
Forces communication and 'Second F' under stress.
