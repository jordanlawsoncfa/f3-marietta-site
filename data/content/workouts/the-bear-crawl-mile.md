### Definition
A grueling transport workout where the PAX must cover a large distance on all fours.

### How it's done
Typically not a full mile, but a 'Beatdown' version involving segments of Bear Crawls interrupted by reps of Merkins or Squats.

### Variations
- Crawl Bear Mile

### Notes
Tests the shoulders and mental grit like nothing else.

### Related terms
- Bear Crawl
