### Definition
A global F3 fitness competition held over four weeks to test the Nation's strength.

### How it's done
Each week in September, a specific, standardized workout is released. All PAX perform it, usually OYO (On Your Own), and submit their scores or times to a national leaderboard.

### Variations
- Mini-IronPax

### Notes
The 'Super Bowl' of F3 fitness.
