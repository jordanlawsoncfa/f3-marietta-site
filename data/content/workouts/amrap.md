### Definition
As Many Reps (or Rounds) As Possible within a set time limit.

### How it's done
The Q sets a timer (e.g., 10 minutes). The PAX move through a circuit of exercises as fast as possible, completing as many rounds as they can before the 'Omaha' is called.

### Variations
- AMReps

### Notes
Focuses on individual maximum effort.
