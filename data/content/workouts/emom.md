### Definition
A high-intensity structure where a specific task must be completed within a 60-second window.

### How it's done
The Q sets a timer. At the start of every minute, the PAX perform a set number of reps (e.g., 5 Burpees). Any time remaining in that minute is used for rest. The next set starts exactly at the 60-second mark.

### Variations
- E2MOM (Every 2 Minutes)

### Notes
Tests efficiency and recovery speed.

### Related terms
- AMRAP
