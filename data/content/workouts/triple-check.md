### Definition
A three-man rotation workout where one man is the 'timer' and two are working.

### How it's done
PAX group into threes. P1 performs Exercise A, P2 performs Exercise B, and P3 runs a lap. When P3 returns, they rotate positions. The 'Check' is complete when all three have run.

### Variations
- Quadruple Check

### Notes
Keeps the group moving with zero standing around.
