### Definition
A detailed look at how a Q would execute an 11s beatdown.

### How it's done
**The Setup:** Point A is the Flag; Point B is the first light pole. **The Work:** 10 Burpees at the Flag, sprint to the pole for 1 Squat. Sprint back for 9 Burpees and 2 Squats. **The Finish:** Continue until you hit 1 Burpee and 10 Squats. **Omaha:** Call if time is low.
