### Definition
A workout centered entirely around the use of a cinder block (coupon).

### How it's done
The PAX stay in one location and perform a series of heavy compound movements (e.g., 20 Block Thrusters, 20 Block Rows, 20 Block Chest Presses). Minimal running, maximum weight.

### Variations
- Blockees

### Notes
Develops 'Old Man Strength.'
