### Definition
A lubricant stick used to prevent skin irritation during movement.

### How it's used
Apply to areas prone to chafing (thighs, underarms) before a long run or ruck.

### Variations
- Vaseline
- Trail Toes

### Notes
Essential for any event over 60 minutes.

- CSAUP
