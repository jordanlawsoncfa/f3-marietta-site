### Definition
The symbol of an F3 AO. A physical flag mounted on a shovel handle.

### How it's used
Planted at the start of every workout to mark the AO. It never touches the ground.

### Variations
- Fiberglass
- Wood handle

### Notes
Building your first shovel flag is a rite of passage for a Site Q.
