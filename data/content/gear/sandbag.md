### Definition
A heavy-duty bag filled with sand, used for 'sandbag' workouts.

### How it's used
Used for carries, tosses, and weighted PT. Much more dynamic than a coupon.

### Variations
- 40lb
- 60lb
- 80lb

### Notes
GORUCK and Rep Fitness make the best ones.
