### Definition
Running shoes with aggressive tread for off-road use.

### How it's used
Use these for AOs that are primarily grass, mud, or trail-based.

### Variations
- Gortex
- Non-waterproof

### Notes
Gortex keeps feet dry but can trap water inside.
