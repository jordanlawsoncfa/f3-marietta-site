### Definition
The standard F3 hand protection. Cheap, durable, and grippy.

### How it's used
Wear them during any workout to protect your hands from pavement, gravel, and wet grass during Merkins or Bear Crawls.

### Variations
- Nitrile-Dipped
- Insulated

### Notes
Go to a hardware store and buy the 5-pack. They are the F3 'uniform.'

- Merkin
