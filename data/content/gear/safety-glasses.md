### Definition
Clear glasses worn to protect eyes from debris.

### How it's used
Used during 'Shovel Flag' construction or certain rugged CSAUP events.

### Variations
- Clear
- Tinted

### Notes
Don't lose an eye to a rogue PVC shard.
