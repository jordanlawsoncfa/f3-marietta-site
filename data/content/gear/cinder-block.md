### Definition
The primary weighted object used in F3 workouts. Standard 8x8x16 size.

### How it's used
Used for overhead presses, squats, curls, and rows. Usually weighs around 35-40 lbs.

### Variations
- Lightweight block
- Paver

### Notes
Every man should eventually own a 'Cindy.'
