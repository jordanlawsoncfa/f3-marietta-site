### Definition
A vest designed to hold weight plates close to the body.

### How it's used
Used for 'Murph' training or to increase the intensity of bodyweight moves.

### Variations
- 5.11
- TacTec

### Notes
Provides a more balanced weight than a ruck.

### Related terms
- The Murph
