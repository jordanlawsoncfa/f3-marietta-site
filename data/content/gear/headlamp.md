### Definition
A hands-free light source worn on the forehead.

### How it's used
Essential for seeing in the pre-dawn 'Gloom,' especially during runs or rucks.

### Variations
- Red light mode
- LED

### Notes
Get one with a red-light mode so you don't blind your brothers.

### Related terms
- The Gloom
