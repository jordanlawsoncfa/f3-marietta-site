### Definition
A small-range abdominal crunch.

### How it's done
1. On back, knees up at 90 degrees
2. Lift shoulder blades slightly off ground while looking at the sky

### Variations
- Big Boy Sit-ups
- WWIIs

### Notes
Keep chin off chest.

### Related terms
- Big Boy Sit-up
