### Definition
A 4-count core burner on your six.

### How it's done
1. On back, hands under glutes
2. Lift feet 6 inches
3. Alternate kicking legs (R, L, R, 1...)

### Variations
- Hello Dollies
- Rosalitas

### Notes
Keep legs straight.
