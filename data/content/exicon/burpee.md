### Definition
A full-body explosive movement.

### How it's done
1. Squat down
2. Kick feet back to plank
3. Perform Merkin
4. Jump feet in
5. Explosive jump up with hands high

### Variations
- 8-count Burpee
- Peter Parker Burpee

### Notes
The King of F3.
