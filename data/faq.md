# Frequently Asked Questions

## Who is F3 for?
F3 is for men of all fitness levels. We have men in their 20s and men in their 70s. We have ultra-marathoners and men who are just getting off the couch. The only requirement is that you are a male.

## What does it cost?
F3 is always free. There are no membership fees, no dues, and no catch. We are a peer-led organization, so we don't pay instructors or rent gym space.

## What should I bring?
Wear workout clothes appropriate for the weather. We recommend bringing a pair of workout gloves (gardening or mechanic gloves work great) since we often do exercises on pavement. Bring a water bottle.

## Do I need to sign up?
No. You just show up. Check the Locations page to find a workout near you and just be there a few minutes before the start time.

## Is it a religious group?
F3 stands for Fitness, Fellowship, and Faith. However, we define "Faith" not as a specific religion, but as a belief in something bigger than yourself. We are open to men of all faiths and no faith. We end each workout with a "Circle of Trust" where men can share a prayer, a quote, or just a thought, but participation is voluntary.
